## Project Overview
This repository serves as my comprehensive Digital Literacy Portfolio, created as part of my role as a Student Digital Ambassador for the CSE0001 course. The goal of this project is to build, document, and share essential digital skills—ranging from creating professional online profiles to communicating safely and responsibly in the digital world. 

All work is divided into five module-based tasks, accompanied by a final project report reflecting on the learnings from each module.

---

## Repository Structure & Module Summaries

### [Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic](./task-1-presentation/)
**Module 1 summary:** Understanding the core concepts of digital literacy.
* **Contents:** A one-page visual infographic designed to help peers navigate digital tools, maintain a professional online presence, and practice safe internet habits. 

### [Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio](./task-2-portfolio/)
**Module 2 summary:** Building a foundational, professional digital footprint.
* **Contents:** Screenshots documenting the setup of essential professional platforms, including this GitHub repository, LinkedIn, and Kaggle.

### [Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms](./task-3-platforms/)
**Module 3 summary:** Exploring online tools for studying, practicing code, and collaborating on data.
* **Contents:** Proof of completion for a beginner-level coding challenge on HackerRank/CodeChef, alongside collaborative data collection tools.

### [Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide](./task-4-email-etiquette/)
**Module 4 summary:** Mastering professional communication and responsible social media use.
* **Contents:** Drafts of professional emails (requesting an extension and an internship inquiry) and a "Do's and Don'ts" checklist for responsible social media behavior as a college student.

### [Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention](./task-5-cybercrime/)
**Module 5 summary:** Recognizing online threats and protecting digital identities.
* **Contents:** A detailed case study on a common cybercrime (e.g., UPI Fraud/Phishing) and an actionable prevention checklist including national reporting helplines.
